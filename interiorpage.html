Our Surf Lessons

Beginner Surf Lessons.

Lessons Structures.

The first phase of any types of surf lessons is to understand the basic of the safety, therefore we will spend the first 15mins, learning and understanding how the surf actually works! understanding how to locate the Rip Current, observing how the tide works and how can you read the waves direction.

Next Step.

Next, we will introduce the surfboards functionality and how to handle it in the water, as we all know by far, the most common mistakes in surfing that can cost you an injury are coming from your own equipment. it is really important to firstly clear understand about how to handle the surfboards and how to complete control over it.

We will then teach you how to do basic popping up on the surfboards, teach you how to correct standup position, how to balance,how to stop the board properly & how to get off from the board in safety way.

Practical Guide.

Once we done with all of the necessary basic beach practical, we will guid you towards the sea and help you catch your very first small waves, gives you all the necessary feedback, and support. by the end of the day our goal is to make sure that you can get the most out of the entire course with us.

Intermediate Surf Lessons.

Surfing Program.

For our intermediate classes at AlphaZero Surf Bali we require you to be comfortable getting out back to the green waves in small conditions. We run these sessions with a coaching philosophy rather than teaching lessons, and build it around you. We note and correct mistakes throughout the lessons to ensure you progress quickly.

Before we go down to the water we will quickly revisit the basics covered in our Beginner lessons and then move on to explaining intermediate maneuvers such as how to turn. The rest of the lesson will be spent in the water out back, putting into practice what we have covered on land. Techniques we cover in intermediate classes you can read below

Intermediate surf skills cover.

  • Angled take offs and trimming
  • Forehand and backhand turns
  • Generation of speed
  • Surf rules & etiquette
  • Correct wave positioning for take off

Once comfortable with these, we suggest moving on to our advanced level coaching, or a trip out to a different break!
